# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Superstore Sales Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze business performance between **2014 and 2017**.

The dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor sales, profit, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and regional trends through interactive visualizations.

The primary objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance
- Identify high-performing regions
- Discover profitable product categories
- Detect loss-making products
- Analyze customer purchasing patterns
- Support data-driven business decisions

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 🌎 Regional Performance

- West generated the highest sales and profit.
- South recorded the lowest sales performance.

---

### 📦 Product Performance

- Technology is the most profitable category.
- Furniture generates high sales but lower profitability.
- Tables and Bookcases are loss-making products.
- Copiers and Phones contribute the highest profit.

---

### 📈 Sales Trend

Sales consistently increased between **2014 and 2017**, indicating continuous business growth.

---

### 👥 Customer Insights

A small group of customers contributes significantly to total sales, highlighting opportunities for customer retention and loyalty programs.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Reduce discounts on Tables and Bookcases.
- Increase investment in Technology products.
- Improve sales strategy in the South region.
- Investigate low profitability in the Central region.
- Develop loyalty programs for high-value customers.
